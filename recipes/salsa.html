So... you wanna make some salsa??

To make some juicy and hot salsa you will need:

  • 5 medium roma tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Half a red onion
  • 3 to 6 serrano peppers depending on your preferred spice level
  • Half a bundle of cilantro

Wash your ingredients, chop off the stems of your peppers and wrap your garlic in foil.

Roast your tomatoes, foiled garlic, red onion and serrano's at 425 degrees for 30 minutes or until your tomatoes develop a char on top.

A darker char = darker salsa.

Blend the ingredients and eat with the biggest chip you can find.
