Mm mm, some good and refreshing pesto

This is a sweet little pesto recipe which can be used on many things.

It pairs very well with fettuccine or a bowtie pasta as a side to a protein like roasted salmon or chicken


What you will need:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts (can sub chopped walnuts or use a mix of both)
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Salt to taste (about 1/4 a teaspoon)
  • Pepper to taste (about 1/8 teaspoon)

Simply blend/food processor all ingredients except for your salt, oil and pepper.

Once emulsified, slowly add your olive oil to prevent your pesto from separating.

Add your salt to taste, if you add at the end you reduce the risk of over-salting.
